About me

Hello, friends! Thanks for taking the time to learn more about me! My name is Lacey and I'm the owner of Lacey's Beauty Bar. I attended beauty school at North Hills Beauty Academy and became a licensed esthetician. I've been a licensed esthetician for the past 4 years. Ever since I was a little girl, I knew I wanted to be in the industry. I love having a profession that I truly enjoy! I specialize in eyelash extensions, facials, eyebrow waxing, tinting & henna, dermaplane and other body waxing. I cherish the opportunity that I have getting to know my clients, which has allowed me to develop many close friendships! Outside of work, I stay busy raising my two children, Scarlett & Miles. Being a mother has taught me many valuable lessons and I wouldn't trade it for the world! I look forward to meeting you and learning more about your beauty desires!
